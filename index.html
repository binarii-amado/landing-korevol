The Problem

Obligations aren't connected to
operations.

Most regulated organizations operate with compliance fragmented across PDFs, spreadsheets, emails, and disconnected tools. Every obligation exists — but none of them execute.

Every norm, contract, audit, and process can be described — so it can also be converted into software.

How it works

Five steps from norm to
running module.

KoreVol converts any regulation, internal process, or operational obligation into a fully executable, traceable, no-code module — in a fraction of the time it takes to build custom software.

Platform walkthrough · 3 min
01

Semantic Capture

Upload a regulation, policy, contract, standard, or describe a business flow in plain language. KoreVol parses the content semantically to extract its structure.

02

Operational Decomposition

KoreVol identifies obligations, required data, owners, states, evidence needs, controls, deadlines, and outputs — automatically decomposed into operationalizable components.

03

No-Code Module Builder

Convert that structure into forms, intelligent documents, rules, approval flows, and reusable modules — entirely without programming. Edit and adapt in real time.

04

Continuous Execution

The system guides users, assigns tasks, collects evidence, tracks deadlines, and keeps the operational record live — automatically, every day.

05

Audit-Ready Analytics

KoreVol analyzes risks, anomalies, compliance gaps, and evidence quality — generating inspection-ready reports, audit trails, and continuous fraud signals.

Platform Architecture

One core. Unlimited
vertical modules.

KoreVol is built as a horizontal platform with vertical depth. The same core technology powers every module — each vertical module adds specialized domain logic, templates, regulatory maps, and pre-built evidence structures.

KoreFlow
Process Engine

No-code workflow engine built on deterministic finite automata. Model any lifecycle — contracts, audits, inspections, permits, certifications — with full state control.

  • Deterministic state machines
  • Multi-actor approval flows
  • Conditional branching
  • Deadline & escalation engine
KoreDocs
Intelligent Documents

Documents that are not passive files. They capture data, execute logic, validate information, change state, and produce structured outputs automatically.

  • Data-capturing smart forms
  • Logic execution inside docs
  • Auto-generated structured outputs
  • Version-controlled evidence
KoreReg
Regulation-as-Code

Upload any regulation, standard, policy, or certification requirement. KoreReg translates it into controls, tasks, evidence requirements, forms, and audit trails.

  • Semantic norm parsing
  • Auto-generated control matrices
  • CMMC · HIPAA · FAR · BSA ready
  • Living normative maps
KoreAudit
Audit Automation

Pre-audit preparation, continuous evidence collection, parametric balance modeling, risk assessment workflows, and due diligence automation — all in one engine.

  • ISA 315 / NIA-ES 315 aligned
  • Parametric balance sheets
  • Evidence gap analysis
  • Audit trail generation
KoreRisk
Risk & Fraud Analytics

Deterministic mathematical fraud detection — not black-box AI. Benford's Law, duplicate detection, round-number analysis, risk scoring, and anomaly alerts.

  • Benford's Law applied
  • Duplicate & round-number flags
  • Weighted risk matrices
  • Real-time SAR signals
KoreHub
Enterprise Filesystem

Document repository with permissions, full traceability, cloud sync (Google Drive, OneDrive, S3, SharePoint), and a structured evidence vault per process.

  • Granular role permissions
  • Cloud sync & governance
  • Evidence vault per workflow
  • External auditor access
